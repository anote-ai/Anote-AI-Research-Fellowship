## Per-Speaker Pre-Talk Checklist

Copy this checklist for each confirmed speaker.

### Speaker: [Name] — Talk #[N] on [Date]

#### 4 Weeks Before Talk
- [ ] Confirm speaker date, time (Fri 1pm ET), and location (WeWork NYC / Zoom link)
- [ ] Collect speaker bio and headshot for social media
- [ ] Collect talk title and 2–3 sentence abstract
- [ ] Send speaker prep materials (see template below)
- [ ] Get recording consent signed / verbal confirmation
- [ ] Add to social media calendar (Monday spotlight post)
- [ ] Add to internal Slack #fellowship-announcements

#### 1 Week Before Talk
- [ ] Send calendar invite reminder to speaker
- [ ] Confirm AV setup: projector, HDMI, microphone, Zoom link live
- [ ] Share Zoom link with all fellows and staff
- [ ] Confirm recording setup (Zoom cloud record or phone backup)
- [ ] Brief fellows on speaker background and suggested questions
- [ ] Post speaker spotlight on LinkedIn / Twitter (Monday before)

#### Day Of Talk (Fri 1pm)
- [ ] AV test at 12:45pm
- [ ] Zoom recording started before speaker begins
- [ ] Introduce speaker (2–3 sentences: name, affiliation, topic)
- [ ] Facilitate Q&A (15–20 min)
- [ ] Thank speaker publicly in front of fellows
- [ ] Collect any slides from speaker

#### After Talk
- [ ] Send thank-you note within 24 hours (see template below)
- [ ] Upload recording to shared drive
- [ ] Edit / clip highlight for YouTube
- [ ] Post post-talk recap on LinkedIn / Twitter
- [ ] Update pipeline table: Recording Link + YouTube Link
- [ ] Share recording link in Slack with fellows

## Speaker Prep Materials Template

> Send to confirmed speakers ~4 weeks before their talk.

**Subject:** Speaker Prep — Anote AI Fellowship Talk on [Date]

Hi [Speaker Name],

Thank you so much for agreeing to speak at the Anote AI Research Fellowship! Here are the details for your session:

**Session Details**
- Date: Friday, [Date]
- Time: 1:00 – 2:00pm ET
- Location: WeWork NYC ([address]) + Zoom (link below)
- Format: ~35 min talk + ~20 min Q&A
- Zoom Link: [link]

**Audience Profile**
- Up to 15 early-career AI researchers and engineers
- Backgrounds: CS, ML, NLP, data science, software engineering
- Currently: conducting original research + building AI products
- Target venues: NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI

**Suggested Talk Themes** _(feel free to adapt to your own work)_
- Your research journey and how you got into AI
- A deep dive on a specific paper or project
- Advice for early-career AI researchers / practitioners
- The future of [your field]

**Logistics**
- Please bring your own laptop or let us know if you need AV support
- HDMI and adapters available; projector screen provided
- We will record with your permission and post to YouTube
- Please send us your bio (2–3 sentences) and a headshot for our social media post

**Recording Consent**
By participating, you consent to the talk being recorded and shared on Anote's YouTube channel and social media. If you have any restrictions, please let us know.

Looking forward to having you!

[Your Name]
