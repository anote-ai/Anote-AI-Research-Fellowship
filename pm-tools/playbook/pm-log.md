# PM Decision & Lessons Log

Running log for the PM to capture decisions, blockers, lessons, and anything the next fellowship PM should know.

**Instructions:** Add an entry every Friday after the talk. Include the week number, what happened, what you decided, and what you'd do differently.

---

## How to Use This Log

This log becomes the core of the **Fellowship PM Playbook** handed off at the end of the summer. Write it like you're briefing your replacement. Be specific — "speaker confirmed via LinkedIn DM" is more useful than "speaker outreach done."
