# Speaker Outreach Kit

Templates for sourcing, booking, and thanking guest speakers for the Friday Fellowship Talks.

**Confirm each speaker at least 4 weeks in advance.**

---

## 1. Cold Outreach Email (LinkedIn or Email)

**Subject:** Guest Speaker Invitation — Anote AI Research Fellowship (Summer 2026)

```
Hi [First Name],

I'm [PM Name], the fellowship PM at Anote AI. We're running the Anote AI Research Fellowship this summer — a 10-week research program in NYC where we bring together student researchers to publish AI papers and contribute to our product suite.

Each Friday from June through July, we host a guest speaker talk (1–2 PM EST, hybrid: WeWork NYC + Zoom) for our fellows. Past topics have spanned LLM evaluation, enterprise AI, retrieval-augmented generation, and synthetic data.

We'd love to have you speak on [specific topic relevant to their work]. The format is a 40-minute talk + 15-minute Q&A with our cohort of AI researchers and engineers. We record and share all talks on YouTube.

Would you be open to joining us on [proposed date]? Happy to be flexible on timing.

Best,
[PM Name]
Anote AI Research Fellowship PM
[email] | anote.ai
```

## 3. Speaker Confirmation Email (send once they agree)

**Subject:** Confirmed! Anote AI Fellowship Talk — [Date] | Prep Details Inside

```
Hi [First Name],

Wonderful — we're thrilled to have you! Here are the details for your talk:

DATE: Friday, [Month Day], 2026
TIME: 1:00–2:00 PM EST (talk 1:00–1:40, Q&A 1:40–1:55)
FORMAT: [In-person at WeWork NYC, [address] OR Zoom only]
ZOOM LINK: [link]

YOUR AUDIENCE:
[X] AI research fellows — graduate students and engineers from [universities]. They're working on topics including [research tracks]. Smart, curious, and technical.

WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU (1 week before your talk):
- [ ] Slide deck (Google Slides, PDF, or PowerPoint — we'll share-screen)
- [ ] 2–3 sentence bio for your introduction
- [ ] Preferred talk title
- [ ] Recording consent: [Yes / No / Yes with edits before publishing]

LOGISTICS:
- We'll start a Zoom recording at 12:55 PM — let me know if you have any concerns
- Talks are uploaded to the Anote YouTube channel: [link]
- If traveling to WeWork NYC: [address, floor, room] — I'll meet you at reception at 12:30 PM

Please feel free to reach out with any questions. Looking forward to it!

Best,
[PM Name]
[email] | [phone]
```
