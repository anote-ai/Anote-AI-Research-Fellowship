# Friday Fellowship Talk — Run Sheet

**Session:** Every Friday, 1:00–2:00 PM EST | WeWork NYC + Zoom

---

## Run Sheet Template

Copy and fill this out for each talk. Save as `talk-[N]-[speaker-lastname].md` in `pm-tools/talks/`.

```
FRIDAY FELLOWSHIP TALK — Talk #[N]
Date: Friday, [Month Day], 2026
Speaker: [Name, Title, Affiliation]
Topic: [Talk title]
Room: WeWork NYC — [Room name/floor]
Zoom: [link]

TIMELINE
12:00 PM — PM arrives at WeWork, sets up AV/recording
12:30 PM — Speaker arrives (or joins Zoom for remote)
12:45 PM — Do a mic/camera/screen share check
12:55 PM — Start Zoom recording; start room recording
1:00 PM — Welcome fellows, introduce speaker (PM does intro)
1:00–1:40 PM — Speaker talk
1:40–1:55 PM — Q&A (PM moderates, calls on fellows)
1:55 PM — Thank speaker, announce next week's talk
2:00 PM — Stop recording; informal networking
2:15 PM — PM wraps up and sends speaker out

POST-SESSION (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Download Zoom recording
- [ ] Download room AV recording (if in-person)
- [ ] Upload to shared drive: [link] → Talks / Talk #[N] — [Speaker]
- [ ] Clip intro/outro, add title card in iMovie or CapCut
- [ ] Upload to Anote YouTube with title: "Anote AI Fellowship Talk #[N]: [Topic] | [Speaker Name]"
- [ ] Add to YouTube playlist: "Anote AI Research Fellowship — Summer 2026"
- [ ] Share link in #fellowship-talks Slack channel
- [ ] Send thank-you email to speaker (use speaker-confirmation-kit.md thank-you template)
- [ ] Post LinkedIn announcement (use social-media-posts.md template)
```

---

## Speaker Introduction Script

PM reads this at 1:00 PM sharp:

```
Good [afternoon], everyone — welcome to the Anote AI Research Fellowship Talk, week [N] of our summer series.

I'm [PM name], the fellowship PM, and I'm joined today by our cohort of [X] fellows working across AI research and product development here at Anote.

Today we're thrilled to have [Speaker Name], [Title] at [Affiliation]. [1–2 sentence bio]. Their talk today is titled "[Talk Title]."

We'll have about 40 minutes for the talk and 15 minutes for Q&A. Please hold questions until the end — I'll call on you.

[Speaker Name], the floor is yours.
```

---

## AV Setup Checklist

Run this before 1:00 PM every Friday:

**In-room (WeWork NYC):**
- [ ] HDMI cable connected to speaker's laptop (or Anote laptop with slide clicker)
- [ ] Room mic on and tested (record a 10-second test clip)
- [ ] In-room camera framing tested (capture speaker + slides)
- [ ] Backup: Zoom recording started on Anote host account
- [ ] Screen sharing working from speaker's machine

**Remote/Zoom:**
- [ ] Zoom meeting open on Anote host account
- [ ] Cloud recording enabled (confirm in Zoom settings)
- [ ] "Record for cloud" started before speaker begins
- [ ] Waiting room disabled for fellows; enabled for public
- [ ] Zoom link posted in #fellowship-talks 15 min before start

---

## Q&A Moderation Notes

- Prefer fellows' questions over external guests
- Keep each question to 1 minute
- If no hands, have 2 backup questions ready:
  1. "What's the most surprising result you've seen in this space?"
  2. "What would you tell a PhD student or early researcher working in [topic]?"
- Wrap Q&A at 1:55 PM even if questions remain — thank the speaker, offer to connect offline
