## Tuesday — AI Research Standup (5:00–5:30 PM EST)

**Format:** Round-robin scrum. Each fellow gets 3 minutes. PM tracks blockers in real time.

```
📋 AI Research Standup — [Date]
Time: 5:00 PM EST | Zoom: [link]

Agenda:
1. Quick wins / progress since last Tuesday (1 min each)
2. What's the plan for this week? (1 min each)
3. Any blockers? (flag for async follow-up)
4. PM announcements (deadlines, speakers, reminders)

Round-robin order: [list fellow names]

Reminder: drop your update in #research-standup Slack by EOD if you can't make it live.
```
