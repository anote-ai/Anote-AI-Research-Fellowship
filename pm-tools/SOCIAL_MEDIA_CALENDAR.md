## Approval Workflow

1. PM drafts post using templates below
2. PM shares draft in Slack #social-media-approvals (or DM to Natan) at least **48 hours before** scheduled publish time
3. Natan reviews and approves, requests edits, or escalates to Anote marketing
4. PM schedules or publishes post at approved time
5. PM logs post in the weekly table below
