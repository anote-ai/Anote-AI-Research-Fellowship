## 1. Program Overview

The Anote AI Research Fellowship is a competitive summer fellowship for early-career AI researchers and engineers. Fellows spend 10 weeks in New York City conducting original research, owning an AI product, and developing as practitioners.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Duration | Jun 1 – Aug 10 (10 weeks) |
| Location | New York City (WeWork + remote) |
| Fellows | Up to 15 |
| Products | 10 Anote product repos |
| Deliverables | Research paper + final presentation + product contributions |
| Weekly events | Tue 5pm Research Standup, Thu 5pm Product Standup, Fri 1pm Fellowship Talk |
| Talk series | 9 Friday talks, guest speakers from AI research + industry |

## 2. Recruiting and Onboarding Checklist

### Recruiting (8–12 weeks before program start)

- [ ] Post fellowship application on Anote website, LinkedIn, Twitter
- [ ] Distribute to Cornell / Cornell Tech network
- [ ] Set application deadline (~4 weeks before start)
- [ ] Review applications: evaluate research statement, GitHub, background
- [ ] Conduct 30-min interviews with shortlisted candidates
- [ ] Send acceptance and rejection emails
- [ ] Collect acceptances and waitlist backups (target: 15 fellows, up to 18 accepted in case of drop-off)
- [ ] Collect fellow info: name, email, GitHub handle, LinkedIn, background, research interests

### Onboarding (2 weeks before program start)

- [ ] Send welcome email with program overview, schedule, and expectations
- [ ] Add all fellows to GitHub org: anote-ai
- [ ] Add all fellows to Slack workspace: create #fellows, #research, #product channels
- [ ] Assign each fellow to a product repo (see [CODEBASE_SETUP_OVERVIEW.md](../CODEBASE_SETUP_OVERVIEW.md))
- [ ] Send fellows the [CODEBASE_SETUP_OVERVIEW.md](../CODEBASE_SETUP_OVERVIEW.md)
- [ ] Schedule Week 1 kickoff: program orientation, product intros, research scoping
- [ ] Confirm WeWork access passes for all fellows
- [ ] Set up shared Notion / Google Drive for documents
- [ ] Brief Natan on fellow assignments and backgrounds

### Week 1 Kickoff Agenda

1. Program overview and expectations (Natan)
2. Product assignments and codebase walkthrough (PM)
3. Research scoping: how to choose a topic (Natan)
4. How to run a standup (PM)
5. Fellow introductions (lightning round)
6. Q&A

## 4. Speaker Sourcing Process

Full SOP: [SPEAKER_BOOKING_PROCESS.md](./SPEAKER_BOOKING_PROCESS.md)  
Pipeline Tracker: [SPEAKER_PIPELINE_TRACKER.md](./SPEAKER_PIPELINE_TRACKER.md)

**Summary:**
1. Source candidates via Cornell network, Anote relationships, cold LinkedIn/email
2. Send outreach email (template in SOP doc)
3. Follow up 5 and 10 days after if no response
4. On confirmation: send prep materials + calendar invite
5. 1 week before: AV check, Zoom test, brief fellows
6. Day of: AV test at 12:45pm, record full session
7. Post-talk: thank-you note, upload recording, YouTube post, social recap
