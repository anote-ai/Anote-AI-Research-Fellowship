# Anote AI Research Fellowship — Summer 2026

**June 1 – August 10, 2026 | New York City + Virtual**

---

Anote is an applied AI research company based in New York City. We build AI technology to provide high-quality datasets and evaluations for leading enterprises, federal clients, and model providers.

The Anote AI Research Fellowship is a 10-week summer program bringing together a select cohort of student researchers and engineers to do real, publishable AI research — while directly contributing to Anote's products. Fellows work alongside the Anote team, guided by research mentors and a steering committee, with the goal of shipping meaningful work that lasts beyond the summer.

Fellows join us in person at our WeWork space in NYC every Friday, and participate virtually throughout the week. Remote fellows are welcome.

---

## What Fellows Work On

Every fellow is responsible for three core deliverables by August 10:

| # | Deliverable | Goal |
|---|-------------|------|
| 1 | **AI Research Paper** | Publish a paper to arXiv and submit to a top AI conference (e.g. AAAI 2027). Topics drawn from [anote-ai/Research](https://github.com/anote-ai/Research) or proposed by the fellow. |
| 2 | **Final Presentation** | Present the summer's work on August 5 — recorded and published to Anote's YouTube and website. Slides use the [Anote Template](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1x4E2dwBnDImHjMOMaI8lLWcRNjVWlnsYEJ3WOwlhKuA/edit?usp=sharing), inspired by past showcases like [Pale Blue Dot](https://anote.ai/palebluedot) and [Panacea](https://anote.ai/panacea). |
| 3 | **Product Contribution** | Own one Anote product and meaningfully improve it using Claude Code or Codex. All product repos live at [github.com/anote-ai](https://github.com/orgs/anote-ai/repositories). |

---

## Program Highlights

- **Orientation:** Monday, June 1 — 6:00–8:00 PM EST
- **Weekly Research Standup:** Every Tuesday, 5:00–5:30 PM EST (virtual)
- **Weekly Product Standup:** Every Thursday, 5:00–5:30 PM EST (virtual)
- **Friday Fellowship Talks:** Every Friday, 1:00–2:00 PM EST — guest AI experts, recorded (in-person at WeWork NYC + virtual)
- **Midpoint Check-ins:** Wednesday, July 1 — one-on-one sessions with Natan (5:00–10:00 PM)
- **Final Presentations:** Wednesday, August 5 — recorded and published (5:00–10:00 PM)
- **Anote Summer Party:** Thursday, August 7 — celebrating the fellows in NYC (7:00–10:00 PM)
- **Last Day:** Monday, August 10 — clean up all work, document everything, submit papers
