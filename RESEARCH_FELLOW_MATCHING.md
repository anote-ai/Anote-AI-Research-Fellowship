## Fellow Paper Deliverable Checklist

Every fellow must complete the following by **August 10, 2026**:

- [ ] Topic confirmed with Natan (by June 9 standup)
- [ ] Literature review complete
- [ ] Abstract and outline drafted (by June 23 standup)
- [ ] Methodology and experiments section written (by July 14 standup)
- [ ] Full draft shared with team for feedback (by July 21 standup)
- [ ] Revisions incorporated (by July 28 standup)
- [ ] Paper submitted to arXiv
- [ ] Paper submitted to at least one journal or conference
- [ ] Paper presented at Final Presentations (August 5)
